Total petrol/diesel consumption for car vehicle per month: If you don't know how many litres used each month, please proceed to the following question, which will compute based on distance.
Diesel contains 86.2% carbon, or 720 grammes of carbon per litre of diesel. To convert this carbon to CO2, 1920 grammes of oxygen are required. The total CO2/liter diesel is thus 720 + 1920 = 2640 grammes.

Petrol contains 87 percent carbon, or 652 grammes of carbon per litre of gasoline. To convert this carbon to CO2, 1740 grammes of oxygen are required. The total is 652 + 1740 = 2392 grammes of CO2/liter of gasoline.
