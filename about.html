What is carbon footprint?

The entire amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) produced by our actions is referred to as our carbon footprint. Malaysia's CO2 emissions per capita in 2019 were 7.67 tonnes of CO2. Malaysia's CO2 emissions per capita grew from 1.32 tonnes of CO2 per capita in 1970 to 7.67 tonnes of CO2 per capita in 2019, rising at a 3.79 percent yearly rate.
